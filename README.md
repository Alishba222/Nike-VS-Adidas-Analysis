Nike vs Adidas Competitive Retail Analytics Dashboard

Project Overview:
This repository showcases a Business Intelligence solution designed to compare the market positioning and retail performance of Nike and Adidas. Built using Looker Studio, this dashboard transforms raw CSV data into a strategic tool for analyzing pricing structures, discount frequency, and consumer satisfaction across the global footwear market.

Key Analysis Features:
Brand Record Distribution: Visualizes market volume, highlighting Nike's 72.2 percent dominance in the product catalog.
Pricing Dynamics: A detailed scatter plot correlating sale prices with user ratings to identify value for money trends.
Discount Benchmarking: Comparative analysis of discount strategies across Nike and Adidas sub-brands like Originals, Core Neo, and Sport Performance.
Consumer Sentiment Mapping: Distribution of ratings and review counts to measure brand engagement and product popularity.
Retail Performance Table: A granular look at top-performing products, such as the Women's Adidas Sport series.

Technical Stack:
Dashboard Engine: Looker Studio.
Data Source: Cleaned CSV Dataset.
Techniques Used: Data Blending, Calculated Fields, and Dynamic Filtering.

Business Insights:
Market Presence: Nike maintains a higher volume of record counts, while Adidas segments its strategy across specialized sub-brands.
Pricing Playbook: Adidas Core Neo segments utilize higher discount rates to drive volume compared to Nike's premium pricing strategy.
Rating Consistency: The rating distribution histogram indicates high consumer satisfaction across the mid to high price brackets.
